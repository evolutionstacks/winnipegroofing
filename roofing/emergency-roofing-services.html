Winnipeg’s fluctuating temperatures often leads to frequent roof problems that homeowners should be aware of . These problems include damage from heavy snow loads.

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams form when the roof’s temperature fluctuates, causing snow to melt and refreeze at the edges, leading to leaks .
  • Shingle Damage: Broken or missing shingles are a frequent occurrence, especially after Winnipeg’s notorious windstorms.
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture buildup from condensation or leaks can lead to mold growth, damaging both the roof structure and your health .

Protect Your Roof with Regular Maintenance

Regular inspections is essential to extending your roof’s lifespan. Without consistent care , small problems such as ice dams can become costly repairs.

  • Shingle Replacement: Cracked or missing shingles need to be replaced immediately .
  • Gutter Cleaning: Regular gutter cleaning helps protect the roof from ice dams.
  • Roof Inspections: Scheduling a roof inspection once a year ensures small problems are caught early .

Warning Signs Your Roof Needs Professional Attention

While DIY repairs might seem cost-effective , there are potential dangers to fixing roofing issues yourself. Inexperienced homeowners can risk injuring themselves .

  • There are signs of water damage: stained ceilings are clear indications that water is damaging the underlying structure.
  • Your roof is aging: Roofs over 15 years old often start showing signs of wear and tear .
  • Severe weather: After experiencing strong winds, it’s important to have a professional assess your roof for hidden damage.
