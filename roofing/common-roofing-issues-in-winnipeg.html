Winnipeg’s fluctuating temperatures often leads to typical roofing damage that homeowners should be aware of . These problems include leaks caused by ice dams .

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams form when the roof’s temperature fluctuates, causing snow to melt and refreeze at the edges, leading to leaks .
  • Shingle Damage: Heavy winds can lift or tear shingles, exposing the roof to further damage .
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture buildup from condensation or leaks can lead to mold growth, damaging both the roof structure and your health .

The Importance of Regular Roof Maintenance in Winnipeg

Regular inspections is critical to extending your roof’s lifespan. Without regular upkeep, small problems such as missing shingles can become costly repairs.

  • Shingle Replacement: Cracked or missing shingles need to be replaced immediately .
  • Gutter Cleaning: Clearing debris from gutters can prevent water backup and leaks .
  • Roof Inspections: Professional inspections can help you identify hidden damage before it worsens.

Why You Should Call a Professional Roofer in Winnipeg

While DIY repairs might seem straightforward, there are certain risks to fixing roofing issues yourself. Inexperienced homeowners can risk injuring themselves .

  • There are signs of water damage: peeling paint are clear indications that water is seeping through the roof .
  • Your roof is aging: Roofs over 15 years old often require more frequent repairs .
  • Severe weather: After a major storm , it’s important to have a professional assess your roof for hidden damage.

Key Factors to Consider When Hiring a Roofer

When selecting a contractor, consider experience . A reputable roofing company should provide quality guarantees. Take time to compare to ensure you're making the best decision for your roofing project.
