Choosing the Right Roofing Contractor

Winnipeg’s extreme winter conditions often leads to frequent roof problems that homeowners should be aware of . These issues include damage from heavy snow loads.

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams are a common issue in Winnipeg, particularly after heavy snowfalls.
  • Shingle Damage: Broken or missing shingles are a frequent occurrence, especially after Winnipeg’s notorious windstorms.
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture buildup from condensation or leaks can lead to mold growth, damaging both the roof structure and your health .

Why Regular Roof Inspections are Key in Winnipeg

Regular roof maintenance is essential to avoiding expensive repairs . Without consistent care , small problems such as missing shingles can escalate quickly .

  • Shingle Replacement: Cracked or missing shingles need to be replaced immediately .
  • Gutter Cleaning: Regular gutter cleaning helps protect the roof from ice dams.
  • Roof Inspections: Professional inspections can help you identify hidden damage before it worsens.

Should You Fix It Yourself or Call a Professional?

While handling roof issues on your own might seem straightforward, there are potential dangers to attempting repairs without professional help . untrained individuals can void warranties.

  • There are signs of water damage: Leaks inside the home are clear indications that water is damaging the underlying structure.
  • Your roof is aging: Roofs over 20 years old often start showing signs of wear and tear .
  • Severe weather: After experiencing strong winds, it’s recommended to have a professional assess your roof for hidden damage.
