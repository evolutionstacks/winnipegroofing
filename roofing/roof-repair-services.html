Winnipeg’s harsh weather often leads to typical roofing damage that homeowners should be aware of . These challenges include damage from heavy snow loads.

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams are a common issue in Winnipeg, particularly after heavy snowfalls.
  • Shingle Damage: Broken or missing shingles are a frequent occurrence, especially after Winnipeg’s notorious windstorms.
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture buildup from condensation or leaks can lead to mold growth, damaging both the roof structure and your health .

Why Routine Roof Maintenance Matters

Regular care is essential to keeping your roof in top condition . Without regular upkeep, small problems such as ice dams can lead to significant damage .

  • Shingle Replacement: Cracked or missing shingles need to be replaced immediately .
  • Gutter Cleaning: Clearing debris from gutters can prevent water backup and leaks .
  • Roof Inspections: Scheduling a roof inspection once a year ensures small problems are caught early .

Why You Should Call a Professional Roofer in Winnipeg

While do-it-yourself fixes might seem cost-effective , there are serious drawbacks to fixing roofing issues yourself. Inexperienced homeowners can void warranties.

  • There are signs of water damage: stained ceilings are clear indications that water is seeping through the roof .

How to Select the Right Roofer in Winnipeg

When hiring a roofer , consider credentials . A qualified roofing company should provide quality guarantees. Research thoroughly to ensure you're choosing the right team for your roofing project.

  • Check reviews and ratings: Look at online reviews .
  • Verify licenses and insurance: Make sure the company is bonded.
  • Ask about warranties: Ensure the company offers a warranty on workmanship .
