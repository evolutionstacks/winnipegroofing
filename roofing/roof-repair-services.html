Roof Installation

Winnipeg’s heavy snowfall and rain often leads to typical roofing damage that homeowners should be aware of . These problems include moisture build-up leading to rot and mold .

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams are a common issue in Winnipeg, particularly after heavy snowfalls.
  • Shingle Damage: Heavy winds can lift or tear shingles, exposing the roof to further damage .
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture damage is a serious issue, especially in older homes with inadequate ventilation.

Why Regular Roof Inspections are Key in Winnipeg

Regular care is essential to extending your roof’s lifespan. Without consistent care , small problems such as missing shingles can escalate quickly .

  • Shingle Replacement: Cracked or missing shingles need to be replaced immediately .
  • Gutter Cleaning: Regular gutter cleaning helps protect the roof from ice dams.
  • Roof Inspections: Professional inspections can help you identify hidden damage before it worsens.

Signs You Need a Professional Roofing Contractor

While do-it-yourself fixes might seem tempting , there are serious drawbacks to fixing roofing issues yourself. Inexperienced homeowners can risk injuring themselves .

  • There are signs of water damage: Leaks inside the home are clear indications that water is damaging the underlying structure.
  • Your roof is aging: Roofs over 20 years old often may need a full replacement.
  • Severe weather: After a major storm , it’s recommended to have a professional assess your roof for hidden damage.
