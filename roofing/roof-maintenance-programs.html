Emergency Roofing Services

Winnipeg’s extreme winter conditions often leads to frequent roof problems that homeowners should be aware of . These challenges include damage from heavy snow loads.

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams form when the roof’s temperature fluctuates, causing snow to melt and refreeze at the edges, leading to leaks .
  • Shingle Damage: Heavy winds can lift or tear shingles, exposing the roof to further damage .
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture buildup from condensation or leaks can lead to mold growth, damaging both the roof structure and your health .

The Benefits of Consistent Roof Care

Regular inspections is vital to avoiding expensive repairs . Without routine checks , small problems such as minor leaks can become costly repairs.

  • Shingle Replacement: Cracked or missing shingles need to be replaced immediately .
  • Gutter Cleaning: Regular gutter cleaning helps protect the roof from ice dams.
  • Roof Inspections: Scheduling a roof inspection once a year ensures small problems are caught early .

Should You Fix It Yourself or Call a Professional?

While DIY repairs might seem cost-effective , there are certain risks to fixing roofing issues yourself. Inexperienced homeowners can risk injuring themselves .

  • There are signs of water damage: peeling paint are clear indications that water is damaging the underlying structure.
  • Your roof is aging: Roofs over 20 years old often require more frequent repairs .
  • Severe weather: After a major storm , it’s wise to have a professional assess your roof for hidden damage.
