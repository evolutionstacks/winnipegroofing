Emergency Roofing Services

Winnipeg’s heavy snowfall and rain often leads to frequent roof problems that homeowners should be aware of . These issues include moisture build-up leading to rot and mold .

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams form when the roof’s temperature fluctuates, causing snow to melt and refreeze at the edges, leading to leaks .
  • Shingle Damage: Heavy winds can lift or tear shingles, exposing the roof to further damage .
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture buildup from condensation or leaks can lead to mold growth, damaging both the roof structure and your health .

Why Regular Roof Inspections are Key in Winnipeg

Regular roof maintenance is critical to extending your roof’s lifespan. Without consistent care , small problems such as minor leaks can lead to significant damage .

  • Shingle Replacement: Cracked or missing shingles need to be replaced immediately .
  • Gutter Cleaning: Clearing debris from gutters can prevent water backup and leaks .
  • Roof Inspections: Professional inspections can help you identify hidden damage before it worsens.

Roofing Issues You Shouldn’t Ignore

While DIY repairs might seem cost-effective , there are potential dangers to attempting repairs without professional help . Inexperienced homeowners can void warranties.

  • There are signs of water damage: peeling paint are clear indications that water is damaging the underlying structure.
  • Your roof is aging: Roofs over 20 years old often may need a full replacement.
  • Severe weather: Following a heavy snowstorm , it’s wise to have a professional assess your roof for hidden damage.

How to Choose a Roofing Contractor in Winnipeg

When hiring a roofer , consider credentials . A qualified roofing company should provide transparent pricing . Take time to compare to ensure you're choosing the right team for your roofing project.

  • Check reviews and ratings: Look at customer testimonials .
  • Verify licenses and insurance: Make sure the company is insured .
  • Ask about warranties: Always get a warranty in writing.
