Choosing the Right Roofing Contractor

Trusted Roofing Solutions for Winnipeg Residents

Winnipeg’s harsh weather often leads to typical roofing damage that homeowners should be aware of . These challenges include damage from heavy snow loads.

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams form when the roof’s temperature fluctuates, causing snow to melt and refreeze at the edges, leading to leaks .
  • Shingle Damage: Broken or missing shingles are a frequent occurrence, especially after Winnipeg’s notorious windstorms.
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture damage is a serious issue, especially in older homes with inadequate ventilation.

How Routine Maintenance Can Save You Money

Regular care is vital to avoiding expensive repairs . Without routine checks , small problems such as missing shingles can escalate quickly .

  • Shingle Replacement: Cracked or missing shingles need to be replaced immediately .
  • Gutter Cleaning: Clearing debris from gutters can prevent water backup and leaks .
  • Roof Inspections: Scheduling a roof inspection once a year ensures small problems are caught early .

When Should You Call a Professional Roofer?

While handling roof issues on your own might seem straightforward, there are certain risks to fixing roofing issues yourself. untrained individuals can cause more harm than good .

  • There are signs of water damage: Leaks inside the home are clear indications that water is seeping through the roof .
  • Your roof is aging: Roofs over a decade old often may need a full replacement.
  • Severe weather: After a major storm , it’s recommended to have a professional assess your roof for hidden damage.

Tips for Hiring the Best Roofing Contractor

When selecting a contractor, consider customer reviews. A qualified roofing company should provide quality guarantees. Take time to compare to ensure you're making the best decision for your roofing project.

  • Check reviews and ratings: Look at customer testimonials .
  • Verify licenses and insurance: Make sure the company is bonded.
  • Ask about warranties: Ensure the company offers a warranty on workmanship .
