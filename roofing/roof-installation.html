In need of professional roofing contractors in Winnipeg? Look no further than the top-rated roofing contractors in Winnipeg . We offer a wide range of services , including maintenance , all designed to extend the life of your roof .

Winnipeg’s extreme winter conditions often leads to common roofing issues that homeowners should be aware of . These issues include damage from heavy snow loads.

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams form when the roof’s temperature fluctuates, causing snow to melt and refreeze at the edges, leading to leaks .
  • Shingle Damage: Heavy winds can lift or tear shingles, exposing the roof to further damage .
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture damage is a serious issue, especially in older homes with inadequate ventilation.

Protect Your Roof with Regular Maintenance

Regular inspections is critical to keeping your roof in top condition . Without regular upkeep, small problems such as minor leaks can escalate quickly .

  • Shingle Replacement: Cracked or missing shingles need to be replaced immediately .
  • Gutter Cleaning: Regular gutter cleaning helps protect the roof from ice dams.
  • Roof Inspections: Scheduling a roof inspection once a year ensures small problems are caught early .

Should You Fix It Yourself or Call a Professional?

While DIY repairs might seem cost-effective , there are serious drawbacks to fixing roofing issues yourself. Inexperienced homeowners can void warranties.

  • There are signs of water damage: peeling paint are clear indications that water is damaging the underlying structure.
  • Your roof is aging: Roofs over a decade old often start showing signs of wear and tear .
  • Severe weather: Following a heavy snowstorm , it’s wise to have a professional assess your roof for hidden damage.

Finding the Right Roofers for Your Home

When selecting a contractor, consider credentials . A qualified roofing company should provide licensed services . Take time to compare to ensure you're choosing the right team for your roofing project.

  • Check reviews and ratings: Look at feedback from previous clients.
  • Verify licenses and insurance: Make sure the company is bonded.
  • Ask about warranties: Always get a warranty in writing.
