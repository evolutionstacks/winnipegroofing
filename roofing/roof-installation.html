Roof Maintenance Programs

Winnipeg’s heavy snowfall and rain often leads to typical roofing damage that can quickly worsen if left unattended. These issues include leaks caused by ice dams .

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams form when the roof’s temperature fluctuates, causing snow to melt and refreeze at the edges, leading to leaks .
  • Shingle Damage: Heavy winds can lift or tear shingles, exposing the roof to further damage .
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture damage is a serious issue, especially in older homes with inadequate ventilation.

Protect Your Roof with Regular Maintenance

Regular care is essential to avoiding expensive repairs . Without regular upkeep, small problems such as ice dams can escalate quickly .

  • Shingle Replacement: Replacing damaged shingles as soon as they are spotted is crucial.
  • Gutter Cleaning: Regular gutter cleaning helps protect the roof from ice dams.
  • Roof Inspections: Professional inspections can help you identify hidden damage before it worsens.

How to Know It’s Time to Hire a Professional Roofer

While do-it-yourself fixes might seem straightforward, there are potential dangers to fixing roofing issues yourself. untrained individuals can cause more harm than good .

  • There are signs of water damage: stained ceilings are clear indications that water is seeping through the roof .
  • Your roof is aging: Roofs over 20 years old often require more frequent repairs .
  • Severe weather: After a major storm , it’s wise to have a professional assess your roof for hidden damage.

Best Practices for Hiring Roofing Contractors

When hiring a roofer , consider experience . A reputable roofing company should provide quality guarantees. Research thoroughly to ensure you're choosing the right team for your roofing project.

  • Check reviews and ratings: Look at feedback from previous clients.
  • Verify licenses and insurance: Make sure the company is bonded.
  • Ask about warranties: Ensure the company offers a warranty on workmanship .
