Winnipeg’s heavy snowfall and rain often leads to typical roofing damage that homeowners should be aware of . These challenges include moisture build-up leading to rot and mold .

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams form when the roof’s temperature fluctuates, causing snow to melt and refreeze at the edges, leading to leaks .
  • Shingle Damage: Heavy winds can lift or tear shingles, exposing the roof to further damage .
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture damage is a serious issue, especially in older homes with inadequate ventilation.

The Benefits of Consistent Roof Care

Regular roof maintenance is vital to keeping your roof in top condition . Without consistent care , small problems such as missing shingles can become costly repairs.

  • Shingle Replacement: Replacing damaged shingles as soon as they are spotted is crucial.
  • Gutter Cleaning: Clearing debris from gutters can prevent water backup and leaks .
  • Roof Inspections: Scheduling a roof inspection once a year ensures small problems are caught early .

Signs You Need a Professional Roofing Contractor

While do-it-yourself fixes might seem tempting , there are serious drawbacks to attempting repairs without professional help . untrained individuals can risk injuring themselves .

  • There are signs of water damage: peeling paint are clear indications that water is seeping through the roof .
  • Your roof is aging: Roofs over 15 years old often may need a full replacement.
  • Severe weather: After a major storm , it’s wise to have a professional assess your roof for hidden damage.

Finding the Right Roofers for Your Home

When hiring a roofer , consider credentials . A reputable roofing company should provide quality guarantees. Take time to compare to ensure you're making the best decision for your roofing project.

  • Check reviews and ratings: Look at feedback from previous clients.
