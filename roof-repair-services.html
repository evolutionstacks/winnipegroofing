Winnipeg’s heavy snowfall and rain often leads to common roofing issues that homeowners should be aware of . These problems include damage from heavy snow loads.

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams are a common issue in Winnipeg, particularly after heavy snowfalls.
  • Shingle Damage: Broken or missing shingles are a frequent occurrence, especially after Winnipeg’s notorious windstorms.
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture damage is a serious issue, especially in older homes with inadequate ventilation.

Why Routine Roof Maintenance Matters

Regular care is vital to avoiding expensive repairs . Without consistent care , small problems such as missing shingles can escalate quickly .

  • Shingle Replacement: Cracked or missing shingles need to be replaced immediately .
  • Gutter Cleaning: Regular gutter cleaning helps protect the roof from ice dams.
  • Roof Inspections: Scheduling a roof inspection once a year ensures small problems are caught early .

Roofing Issues You Shouldn’t Ignore

While do-it-yourself fixes might seem cost-effective , there are potential dangers to fixing roofing issues yourself. untrained individuals can risk injuring themselves .

  • There are signs of water damage: peeling paint are clear indications that water is damaging the underlying structure.
  • Your roof is aging: Roofs over 15 years old often require more frequent repairs .
  • Severe weather: Following a heavy snowstorm , it’s important to have a professional assess your roof for hidden damage.

Key Factors to Consider When Hiring a Roofer

When selecting a contractor, consider credentials . A reputable roofing company should provide licensed services . Take time to compare to ensure you're making the best decision for your roofing project.

  • Check reviews and ratings: Look at online reviews .
  • Verify licenses and insurance: Make sure the company is insured .
  • Ask about warranties: Ensure the company offers a warranty on workmanship .
