Roof Installation

Winnipeg’s extreme winter conditions often leads to frequent roof problems that can quickly worsen if left unattended. These challenges include moisture build-up leading to rot and mold .

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams form when the roof’s temperature fluctuates, causing snow to melt and refreeze at the edges, leading to leaks .
  • Shingle Damage: Heavy winds can lift or tear shingles, exposing the roof to further damage .
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture damage is a serious issue, especially in older homes with inadequate ventilation.

Why Regular Roof Inspections are Key in Winnipeg

Regular roof maintenance is critical to avoiding expensive repairs . Without consistent care , small problems such as missing shingles can escalate quickly .

  • Shingle Replacement: Cracked or missing shingles need to be replaced immediately .
  • Gutter Cleaning: Regular gutter cleaning helps protect the roof from ice dams.
  • Roof Inspections: Scheduling a roof inspection once a year ensures small problems are caught early .

Signs You Need a Professional Roofing Contractor

While do-it-yourself fixes might seem cost-effective , there are potential dangers to attempting repairs without professional help . untrained individuals can void warranties.

  • There are signs of water damage: Leaks inside the home are clear indications that water is seeping through the roof .
  • Your roof is aging: Roofs over a decade old often may need a full replacement.
  • Severe weather: After experiencing strong winds, it’s wise to have a professional assess your roof for hidden damage.

How to Choose a Roofing Contractor in Winnipeg

When hiring a roofer , consider credentials . A qualified roofing company should provide licensed services . Take time to compare to ensure you're making the best decision for your roofing project.

  • Check reviews and ratings: Look at online reviews .
  • Verify licenses and insurance: Make sure the company is bonded.
  • Ask about warranties: Always get a warranty in writing.
