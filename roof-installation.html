Winnipeg’s harsh weather often leads to frequent roof problems that homeowners should be aware of . These challenges include leaks caused by ice dams .

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams form when the roof’s temperature fluctuates, causing snow to melt and refreeze at the edges, leading to leaks .
  • Shingle Damage: Broken or missing shingles are a frequent occurrence, especially after Winnipeg’s notorious windstorms.
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture damage is a serious issue, especially in older homes with inadequate ventilation.

The Importance of Regular Roof Maintenance in Winnipeg

Regular care is critical to keeping your roof in top condition . Without consistent care , small problems such as missing shingles can escalate quickly .

  • Shingle Replacement: Cracked or missing shingles need to be replaced immediately .
  • Gutter Cleaning: Regular gutter cleaning helps protect the roof from ice dams.
  • Roof Inspections: Scheduling a roof inspection once a year ensures small problems are caught early .

Roofing Issues You Shouldn’t Ignore

While handling roof issues on your own might seem cost-effective , there are serious drawbacks to attempting repairs without professional help . untrained individuals can cause more harm than good .

  • There are signs of water damage: stained ceilings are clear indications that water is damaging the underlying structure.
  • Your roof is aging: Roofs over 20 years old often require more frequent repairs .
  • Severe weather: After a major storm , it’s wise to have a professional assess your roof for hidden damage.
