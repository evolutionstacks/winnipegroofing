Roof Maintenance Programs

Winnipeg’s fluctuating temperatures often leads to typical roofing damage that homeowners should be aware of . These problems include shingle blow-offs during windstorms .

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams are a common issue in Winnipeg, particularly after heavy snowfalls.
  • Shingle Damage: Broken or missing shingles are a frequent occurrence, especially after Winnipeg’s notorious windstorms.
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture buildup from condensation or leaks can lead to mold growth, damaging both the roof structure and your health .

Protect Your Roof with Regular Maintenance

Regular inspections is vital to keeping your roof in top condition . Without routine checks , small problems such as ice dams can become costly repairs.

  • Shingle Replacement: Replacing damaged shingles as soon as they are spotted is crucial.
  • Gutter Cleaning: Regular gutter cleaning helps protect the roof from ice dams.
  • Roof Inspections: Professional inspections can help you identify hidden damage before it worsens.

Why You Should Call a Professional Roofer in Winnipeg

While do-it-yourself fixes might seem straightforward, there are potential dangers to fixing roofing issues yourself. untrained individuals can cause more harm than good .

  • There are signs of water damage: peeling paint are clear indications that water is seeping through the roof .
  • Your roof is aging: Roofs over a decade old often require more frequent repairs .
  • Severe weather: After a major storm , it’s important to have a professional assess your roof for hidden damage.

Best Practices for Hiring Roofing Contractors

When selecting a contractor, consider experience . A reputable roofing company should provide licensed services . Research thoroughly to ensure you're choosing the right team for your roofing project.

  • Check reviews and ratings: Look at feedback from previous clients.
  • Verify licenses and insurance: Make sure the company is bonded.
  • Ask about warranties: Always get a warranty in writing.
