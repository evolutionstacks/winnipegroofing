Innovations in Roofing Technology

Winnipeg’s harsh weather often leads to typical roofing damage that homeowners should be aware of . These problems include damage from heavy snow loads.

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams form when the roof’s temperature fluctuates, causing snow to melt and refreeze at the edges, leading to leaks .
  • Shingle Damage: Broken or missing shingles are a frequent occurrence, especially after Winnipeg’s notorious windstorms.
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture damage is a serious issue, especially in older homes with inadequate ventilation.

Protect Your Roof with Regular Maintenance

Regular inspections is critical to extending your roof’s lifespan. Without routine checks , small problems such as ice dams can escalate quickly .

  • Shingle Replacement: Cracked or missing shingles need to be replaced immediately .
  • Gutter Cleaning: Regular gutter cleaning helps protect the roof from ice dams.
  • Roof Inspections: Professional inspections can help you identify hidden damage before it worsens.

When Should You Call a Professional Roofer?

While do-it-yourself fixes might seem cost-effective , there are certain risks to fixing roofing issues yourself. untrained individuals can void warranties.

  • There are signs of water damage: stained ceilings are clear indications that water is damaging the underlying structure.
  • Your roof is aging: Roofs over 15 years old often may need a full replacement.
  • Severe weather: Following a heavy snowstorm , it’s recommended to have a professional assess your roof for hidden damage.
