Common Roofing Issues in Winnipeg

Introduction to Roofing Services in Winnipeg

In need of skilled roofing contractors in Winnipeg? Look no further than our team of experienced roofers . We offer a wide range of services , including roof repair , all designed to ensure long-lasting durability.

Whether you need a complete roof overhaul , our professionals are here to provide reliable service.

Winnipeg’s heavy snowfall and rain often leads to typical roofing damage that homeowners should be aware of . These challenges include leaks caused by ice dams .

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams form when the roof’s temperature fluctuates, causing snow to melt and refreeze at the edges, leading to leaks .
  • Shingle Damage: Heavy winds can lift or tear shingles, exposing the roof to further damage .
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture buildup from condensation or leaks can lead to mold growth, damaging both the roof structure and your health .

Why Routine Roof Maintenance Matters

Regular care is vital to keeping your roof in top condition . Without routine checks , small problems such as missing shingles can lead to significant damage .

  • Shingle Replacement: Cracked or missing shingles need to be replaced immediately .
  • Gutter Cleaning: Clearing debris from gutters can prevent water backup and leaks .
  • Roof Inspections: Scheduling a roof inspection once a year ensures small problems are caught early .

Signs You Need a Professional Roofing Contractor

While do-it-yourself fixes might seem straightforward, there are certain risks to fixing roofing issues yourself. untrained individuals can cause more harm than good .

  • There are signs of water damage: peeling paint are clear indications that water is damaging the underlying structure.
  • Your roof is aging: Roofs over 20 years old often start showing signs of wear and tear .
  • Severe weather: Following a heavy snowstorm , it’s important to have a professional assess your roof for hidden damage.
