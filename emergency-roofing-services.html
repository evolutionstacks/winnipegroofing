Winnipeg’s heavy snowfall and rain often leads to frequent roof problems that can quickly worsen if left unattended. These challenges include shingle blow-offs during windstorms .

Why Routine Roof Maintenance Matters

Regular care is essential to extending your roof’s lifespan. Without consistent care , small problems such as minor leaks can escalate quickly .

  • Shingle Replacement: Replacing damaged shingles as soon as they are spotted is crucial.
  • Gutter Cleaning: Regular gutter cleaning helps protect the roof from ice dams.
  • Roof Inspections: Professional inspections can help you identify hidden damage before it worsens.
