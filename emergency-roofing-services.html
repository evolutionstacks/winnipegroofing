Winnipeg’s fluctuating temperatures often leads to frequent roof problems that homeowners should be aware of . These challenges include leaks caused by ice dams .

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams form when the roof’s temperature fluctuates, causing snow to melt and refreeze at the edges, leading to leaks .
  • Shingle Damage: Broken or missing shingles are a frequent occurrence, especially after Winnipeg’s notorious windstorms.
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture damage is a serious issue, especially in older homes with inadequate ventilation.

Why Routine Roof Maintenance Matters

Regular roof maintenance is vital to extending your roof’s lifespan. Without regular upkeep, small problems such as missing shingles can become costly repairs.

  • Shingle Replacement: Replacing damaged shingles as soon as they are spotted is crucial.
  • Gutter Cleaning: Regular gutter cleaning helps protect the roof from ice dams.
  • Roof Inspections: Scheduling a roof inspection once a year ensures small problems are caught early .

Warning Signs Your Roof Needs Professional Attention

While do-it-yourself fixes might seem cost-effective , there are potential dangers to attempting repairs without professional help . untrained individuals can cause more harm than good .

  • There are signs of water damage: Leaks inside the home are clear indications that water is seeping through the roof .
  • Your roof is aging: Roofs over a decade old often require more frequent repairs .
  • Severe weather: After experiencing strong winds, it’s important to have a professional assess your roof for hidden damage.
