Winnipeg’s extreme winter conditions often leads to common roofing issues that homeowners should be aware of . These challenges include shingle blow-offs during windstorms .

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams form when the roof’s temperature fluctuates, causing snow to melt and refreeze at the edges, leading to leaks .
  • Shingle Damage: Heavy winds can lift or tear shingles, exposing the roof to further damage .
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture buildup from condensation or leaks can lead to mold growth, damaging both the roof structure and your health .

The Importance of Regular Roof Maintenance in Winnipeg

Regular inspections is essential to extending your roof’s lifespan. Without routine checks , small problems such as missing shingles can escalate quickly .

  • Shingle Replacement: Replacing damaged shingles as soon as they are spotted is crucial.
  • Gutter Cleaning: Clearing debris from gutters can prevent water backup and leaks .
  • Roof Inspections: Scheduling a roof inspection once a year ensures small problems are caught early .

How to Know It’s Time to Hire a Professional Roofer

While DIY repairs might seem straightforward, there are certain risks to fixing roofing issues yourself. Inexperienced homeowners can void warranties.

  • There are signs of water damage: peeling paint are clear indications that water is seeping through the roof .
  • Your roof is aging: Roofs over 15 years old often require more frequent repairs .
  • Severe weather: Following a heavy snowstorm , it’s important to have a professional assess your roof for hidden damage.

Best Practices for Hiring Roofing Contractors

When hiring a roofer , consider credentials . A reputable roofing company should provide quality guarantees. Research thoroughly to ensure you're choosing the right team for your roofing project.
