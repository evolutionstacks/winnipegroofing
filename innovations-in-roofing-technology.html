Choosing the Right Roofing Contractor

Winnipeg’s harsh weather often leads to common roofing issues that can quickly worsen if left unattended. These issues include leaks caused by ice dams .

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams form when the roof’s temperature fluctuates, causing snow to melt and refreeze at the edges, leading to leaks .
  • Shingle Damage: Heavy winds can lift or tear shingles, exposing the roof to further damage .
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture damage is a serious issue, especially in older homes with inadequate ventilation.

Protect Your Roof with Regular Maintenance

Regular inspections is vital to keeping your roof in top condition . Without consistent care , small problems such as minor leaks can escalate quickly .

  • Shingle Replacement: Replacing damaged shingles as soon as they are spotted is crucial.
  • Gutter Cleaning: Clearing debris from gutters can prevent water backup and leaks .
  • Roof Inspections: Professional inspections can help you identify hidden damage before it worsens.

When Should You Call a Professional Roofer?

While handling roof issues on your own might seem tempting , there are certain risks to fixing roofing issues yourself. Inexperienced homeowners can void warranties.

  • There are signs of water damage: stained ceilings are clear indications that water is seeping through the roof .
  • Your roof is aging: Roofs over 15 years old often require more frequent repairs .
  • Severe weather: After a major storm , it’s important to have a professional assess your roof for hidden damage.

Choosing Winnipeg’s Top Roofing Service Provider

When selecting a contractor, consider customer reviews. A reputable roofing company should provide quality guarantees. Take time to compare to ensure you're choosing the right team for your roofing project.
