Emergency Roofing Services

Winnipeg’s harsh weather often leads to frequent roof problems that can quickly worsen if left unattended. These challenges include leaks caused by ice dams .

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams form when the roof’s temperature fluctuates, causing snow to melt and refreeze at the edges, leading to leaks .
  • Shingle Damage: Heavy winds can lift or tear shingles, exposing the roof to further damage .
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture buildup from condensation or leaks can lead to mold growth, damaging both the roof structure and your health .

How Routine Maintenance Can Save You Money

Regular care is vital to extending your roof’s lifespan. Without consistent care , small problems such as minor leaks can escalate quickly .

  • Shingle Replacement: Replacing damaged shingles as soon as they are spotted is crucial.
  • Gutter Cleaning: Clearing debris from gutters can prevent water backup and leaks .
  • Roof Inspections: Professional inspections can help you identify hidden damage before it worsens.

When Should You Call a Professional Roofer?

While handling roof issues on your own might seem straightforward, there are certain risks to fixing roofing issues yourself. untrained individuals can risk injuring themselves .

  • There are signs of water damage: stained ceilings are clear indications that water is damaging the underlying structure.
  • Your roof is aging: Roofs over 20 years old often require more frequent repairs .
  • Severe weather: Following a heavy snowstorm , it’s recommended to have a professional assess your roof for hidden damage.
