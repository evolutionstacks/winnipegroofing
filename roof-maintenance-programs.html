Emergency Roofing Services

Winnipeg’s harsh weather often leads to typical roofing damage that can quickly worsen if left unattended. These problems include shingle blow-offs during windstorms .

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams form when the roof’s temperature fluctuates, causing snow to melt and refreeze at the edges, leading to leaks .
  • Shingle Damage: Heavy winds can lift or tear shingles, exposing the roof to further damage .
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture damage is a serious issue, especially in older homes with inadequate ventilation.

The Importance of Regular Roof Maintenance in Winnipeg

Regular roof maintenance is essential to avoiding expensive repairs . Without routine checks , small problems such as ice dams can escalate quickly .

  • Shingle Replacement: Cracked or missing shingles need to be replaced immediately .
  • Gutter Cleaning: Regular gutter cleaning helps protect the roof from ice dams.
  • Roof Inspections: Professional inspections can help you identify hidden damage before it worsens.

Warning Signs Your Roof Needs Professional Attention

While do-it-yourself fixes might seem cost-effective , there are certain risks to attempting repairs without professional help . untrained individuals can void warranties.

  • There are signs of water damage: Leaks inside the home are clear indications that water is seeping through the roof .
  • Your roof is aging: Roofs over 15 years old often require more frequent repairs .
  • Severe weather: After a major storm , it’s important to have a professional assess your roof for hidden damage.
