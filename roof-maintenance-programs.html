Winnipeg’s fluctuating temperatures often leads to frequent roof problems that homeowners should be aware of . These challenges include shingle blow-offs during windstorms .

  • Leaks and Ice Dams: Ice dams form when the roof’s temperature fluctuates, causing snow to melt and refreeze at the edges, leading to leaks .
  • Shingle Damage: Heavy winds can lift or tear shingles, exposing the roof to further damage .
  • Moisture & Mold: Moisture buildup from condensation or leaks can lead to mold growth, damaging both the roof structure and your health .

Why Routine Roof Maintenance Matters

Regular care is vital to avoiding expensive repairs . Without routine checks , small problems such as minor leaks can lead to significant damage .

  • Shingle Replacement: Cracked or missing shingles need to be replaced immediately .
  • Gutter Cleaning: Clearing debris from gutters can prevent water backup and leaks .
  • Roof Inspections: Scheduling a roof inspection once a year ensures small problems are caught early .

When Should You Call a Professional Roofer?

While DIY repairs might seem cost-effective , there are potential dangers to fixing roofing issues yourself. untrained individuals can risk injuring themselves .

  • There are signs of water damage: stained ceilings are clear indications that water is seeping through the roof .
  • Your roof is aging: Roofs over a decade old often start showing signs of wear and tear .
  • Severe weather: Following a heavy snowstorm , it’s important to have a professional assess your roof for hidden damage.

How to Select the Right Roofer in Winnipeg

When hiring a roofer , consider experience . A qualified roofing company should provide quality guarantees. Research thoroughly to ensure you're making the best decision for your roofing project.

  • Check reviews and ratings: Look at feedback from previous clients.
  • Verify licenses and insurance: Make sure the company is bonded.
  • Ask about warranties: Ensure the company offers a warranty on workmanship .
